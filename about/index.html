How We Began


JUMP Robotics was established in the fall of 2019 when a group of freshmen decided to pursue their love for robotics. Initially working out of public libaries, backyards, and garages, we have since grown into a fully fledged, competitive team.

As the team has grown in size and overcome obstacles like COVID-19, we have continued to carry that same spirit of passion and drive in every aspect of our work.

With that same spirit, we consistently compete in the playoffs of every single competition in our history
